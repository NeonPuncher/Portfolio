Shadow Beta

Game Description

A 2D RPG hacker simulation game, explore and hack your way to the top of the city!

You're a hacker that lives in the futuristic city of [Elysium] and you've received an e-mail of the prestine hacker academy for only the top level of hackers. It is your job to get yourself into the academy by becoming a pacifist hacker or a genocide hacker. Choose your way to solve problems and learn about the different types of hacking exist and are commonly used in the real world as well. Find unique NPCs you can interact with that tell you about the world and its people to find out how to get into the academy.

Will you become a good hacker? Or will you fall to the dark side of hackers and plunge the world into chaos with yourself at the top? The choice is completely yours.

Development

This game was custom made in partnership.

The idea was that we had to introduce the player to the world and hacking.

I focussed mostly on Concept Arting and creating all the sprites for the game.

The Entire project was made in Unity3D and code editor Visual Studio 2019
