Merchant Simulator

Merchant Development

Game Description

A casual Simulator Game simulating life as a merchant.

You're a normal merchant living in the medieval times and have a shop somewhere down the road. Many adventurers come through these parts going on epic quests to find treasures and slay monsters. It is your job to equip them well for their journey on ahead by providing them equipment, spells, potions, weapons and more!

Order your priorities by going to the map and selecting parts which provide unique resources so you can craft your gear. The more you provide for these adventurers the more well known your shop will become, and some of them might even share parts of their treasures with you!

Development

Development on Merchant Simulator started in June.

This was a project done in 2 weeks.

Process started with making most of the assets for the game.

These were all pixel assets since these are fast and easy to make.

All the code was self made since this was mostly for teaching myself.

The Entire project was made in Unity3D and code editor Visual Studio 2019, art was done in Photoshop
